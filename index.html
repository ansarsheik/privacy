01What this is

Areeya Social Publisher is a personal automation tool that helps a single creator turn short videos into short-form social posts — without the copy-paste, the resizing, or the late-night uploader queue.

Send a clip to a private Telegram bot. The bot writes captions and overlay text with the OpenAI API, renders a clean 9:16 video, and waits for one tap before publishing to the connected accounts. No signups, no public interface, no other users.

02What it does well

A

AI-written captions & overlays

GPT-4o-mini drafts titles, captions, hashtags, and on-screen overlay text from the video itself — never generic, always grounded in the clip.

9:16

Auto vertical render

FFmpeg crops, formats, and overlays text for Shorts and Reels.

W

Whisper transcripts

Spoken audio is transcribed for accurate, contextual captions.

T

Telegram preview & approve

Every post previews in chat. Nothing publishes until you tap a button — one source of control.

↗

Cross-platform publish

One tap fans the post out to Instagram, YouTube Shorts, Facebook, and TikTok — through each platform's official API.

03How it works

01 / SEND

Drop a clip into Telegram

A private bot receives the raw video over an encrypted chat.

02 / GENERATE

AI writes the words

Vision + Whisper inform the caption, hashtags, and on-screen text.

03 / RENDER

FFmpeg cuts the post

Vertical 9:16 with branded overlay, returned as a preview.

04 / PUBLISH

One tap, every platform

Approve once. The post goes live across IG, YouTube, FB, and TikTok.

04Where it publishes

Each platform is reached through its official API, using the operator's own authorized account. No scraping, no automation against terms.

Instagram YouTube Shorts Facebook TikTok
