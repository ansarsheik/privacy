01What this is

My Life on the Move is a personal automation tool that helps a single creator — me — turn short videos I have recorded myself into short-form social posts for my own accounts on Instagram, YouTube Shorts, Facebook, and TikTok.

I send a video to a private Telegram bot. The bot generates captions and overlay text using the OpenAI API, renders the final video, and publishes it to my own social accounts after I tap to approve. The tool is not offered to other users; it has no signups, no website logins, no public interface beyond this page.

02Privacy policy

This privacy policy describes how My Life on the Move (referred to below as “the app”, “we”, or “our”) handles information when it is used by its operator and how it interacts with the platforms it connects to.

Because the app is a single-operator personal tool, it does not collect information from members of the public. The platforms it publishes to — Instagram, Facebook, YouTube, and TikTok — collect and process data from their viewers according to their own privacy policies, not ours.

What information the app handles

  • Videos and captions you send to the bot. These are stored temporarily on the operator’s own computer, processed through video tooling, and then uploaded to the social platforms you have approved.
  • Generated text (titles, captions, hashtags, overlay text) produced by the OpenAI API based on the topic and video you provide.
  • Access tokens for the social platforms you authorize. These are stored locally on the operator’s computer in protected files. They are never shared, sold, or transmitted to any third party other than the platform that issued them.
  • Operational logs (timestamps of uploads, which post went to which platform, errors). Stored locally in a SQLite database on the operator’s computer.

What the app does not do

  • It does not read messages, posts, friends, follower lists, DMs, or any content belonging to other users on any platform.
  • It does not collect data from visitors to your social profiles.
  • It does not run any tracking, advertising, or analytics on this website.
  • It does not sell, rent, lease, or otherwise share any data with third parties for marketing.
  • It does not store data on cloud servers operated by us. All operational data lives on the operator’s own computer.

Third-party services used

The app integrates with the following services. Each handles data according to its own privacy policy, which you should consult if you want details on their practices.

  • Meta (Instagram & Facebook) Receives the final processed video, caption, and hashtags for publishing. Privacy policy
  • Google (YouTube) Receives the final processed video and metadata for publishing as a YouTube Short. Privacy policy
  • TikTok Receives the final processed video and metadata for publishing. Privacy policy
  • OpenAI Receives the topic plus sampled video frames to generate text. Per OpenAI policy, API data is not used for training. Privacy policy
  • Telegram Hosts the private bot interface used to send videos to the app. Privacy policy

The app’s use of information received from Meta APIs follows the Meta Platform Terms and Developer Policies. Similarly, use of YouTube data follows the YouTube API Services Terms and the Google Privacy Policy, and use of TikTok data follows the TikTok Developer Terms.

How long data is kept

Raw video files received via the bot are kept for up to 30 days on the operator’s computer for retry and debugging, then deleted. Processed videos that have been published are kept for record-keeping. Operational logs are kept indefinitely but contain no third-party personal data. Access tokens are kept for as long as they remain valid; expired tokens are deleted.

Your rights

If you are someone whose data has somehow ended up in this app (for example, you appear in a video I have recorded), you can email the address below to request that I delete the related content and any derived files. Because this is a personal tool with a tiny dataset, requests are usually fulfilled within a few days.

Where applicable under UK GDPR, you also have the right to access information about how your data is processed, to correct it, and to lodge a complaint with the UK Information Commissioner’s Office.

Children

This app is not intended for or directed at children under 13 (or under 16 in the EU/UK). No data about children is knowingly processed.

Security

Access tokens are stored on a single computer protected by full-disk encryption and user-account authentication. Network traffic to and from third-party APIs is encrypted in transit (HTTPS / TLS). No production database is exposed to the internet.

Changes to this policy

If material changes happen, the “Last updated” date at the top of this page will change and a brief summary of what changed will be added below.
